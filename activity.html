    1st UBS Global Quantitative Investment competition

    2018-10
    • The 1st UBS Global Quantitative Investment competition is held by UBS China, aiming to provide college students an oppotunity to get involved in quantitative trading.
      This year, the main topic of the competition is about machine learning strategies in asset management and economic indicatation.
    • Our Team handed in a comprehensive report on constructing macro-economic indicators through machine learning methods. We presented two indicators and a trading strategy based on them, which has an annul return of 40% and a sharpe ratio of 1.31.
      We were rewarded the Final List (top 14 out of 200+ teams).
      My role is to develop, optimize and backtest the trading strategy.
